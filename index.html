The Stupid reason this site exists

So, it was a hot summer evening right after exams ended when I had that boost of motivation to make something. Naturally i hit up ma bro and asked him for an idea for a "simple python script". "Idk if it's possible but a script that will go thru the anime lists in any website (I mean any website of your choice, the user can't change it) and suggests you a random anime" he said. As you would expect I proceeded with making this full blown website after hearing that suggestion.
